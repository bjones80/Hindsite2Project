Youth Employment Program

The Youth Employment Program is an opportunity for high school and college students to learn work skills in the local industry. Secondly Hindsite believes the young workers can provide a service to the local industry by being able to perform seasonal low risk work assignments. I.e. manual weed work, infrastructure mapping (culvert condition, signs, etc.), trash pick-up.

  • noxious and invasive weed and vegetation control through pesticide applications
  • hand removal for cheat grass, henbane, etc.
  • specifically tailored treatments for weed species and location (I.E. roadsides, well sites, construction sites, or other ground disturbance)
